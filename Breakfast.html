What are we making?

by: Ashleigh Knight

My favourite breakfast is scrambled eggs and toast. I make this breakfast everyday because breakfast is life. I make this breakfast because I get hungry at breakfast time.

Ingredients

  • 1 raw egg
  • 2 slices of toast
  • Choice of onions, peppers etc.
  • Oil and pan

What to do

  1. Crack egg into a bowl and scramble
  2. Chop onions and peppers
  3. Heat the pan and add a little oil
  4. Add onions and peppers to the egg
  5. Pour mixture into the pan and scramble the mixture
